About Me

Resume

My name is Luis Flores, a graduating senior pursuing a BS in Electrical Engineering from the University of Illinois at Chicago to be completed May 2015. Born and raised in the vast farmlands of central Mexico, thanks to my passion for hands-on projects, technology and art, I have found a great love for embedded electronics, hardware design and programming. As an intern at Wells-Gardner Electronics, I did general hardware and firmware testing and wrote some Python based tools to expedite the testing workflow. As a Hardware Development Intern at WMS Gaming, I conducted functional testing on new and exciting casino and gaming products and used CAD based tools to design a new meter board to be installed in the newest generation of casino gaming machine cabinets. I also have managerial and leadership experience gained from leading the 22 students that made up the UIC Campus Housing East Side Security Team for two consecutive semesters in 2014. In my spare time I enjoy tinkering with hobby electronics and programming. Be sure to look at my projects page and Github for some examples.

When not working on my technical projects, I enjoy art both in multimedia and traditional formats. I have experience working with the Adobe Creative Suite to create lively and captivating recruiting videos for the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers' UIC Chapter during my Fall and Spring terms as Historian. I was also lead video editor, producer and sound designer for the WMS Gaming intern video project in Summer 2014. I also really enjoy working with simple paper and pencil, drawing the human figure in both western and eastern styles. For music, I love to listen to solo piano works and instrumental hip-hop.

About this site

I'm a big fan of the current minimalist, clean and responsive design trends so I knew that my site had to incorporate these elements. I knew that learning to use the latest web technologies like Javascript, PHP and the Bootstrap framework was the way to go, but as an engineer I knew there had to be a more efficient use of my time. So thanks to StartBootstrap's free templates I was able to build this site in a relatively short amount of time using the skills I already had while learning new skills in the process. The backend is hosted on a DigitalOcean Ubuntu server that I set up and mantain.
